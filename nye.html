University of St. La Salle

The University Of St.La Salle(USLS) is a Catholic private research university run by the De La Salle Brothers, located in Lasalle Avenue, Bacolod, Negros Occidental, Philippines. Established in 1962 as Lasalle College Bacolod, it is the second oldest campus founded by the congregation in the country (first and oldest outside of manila)
